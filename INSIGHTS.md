##  Key Findings & Insights

###  Overall Performance-:
- Hospital treated **55,392 patients** generating total 
  revenue of **$1.42 billion** over 6 years (2019–2024)
- Average billing per patient is **$25,539** with average 
  hospital stay of **15.51 days**
- **34% of all test results are Abnormal** — indicating 
  one-third of patients require clinical follow-up

###  Admission Analysis-:  
- Admissions are almost equally split across all three types:
  Elective **(32.92%)**, Emergency **(33.47%)**, 
  Urgent **(33.61%)** — showing no single type dominates
- 2020 shows visible spike in the trend line — 
  **COVID-19 impact** on hospital admissions clearly visible
- 2024 shows sharp drop — data only covers partial year

###  Clinical Insights-:
- **Asthma** patients have the longest average hospital 
  stay at **15.70 days** — highest bed occupancy cost
- **Arthritis** has the highest abnormal test rate 
  at **34.3%** — requires most diagnostic resources
- **Obesity** drives highest average billing at **$25,806** 
  per patient — chronic condition management is costliest
- **Cancer** has lowest avg billing at **$25,162** despite 
  being a serious condition — suggests shorter treatment episodes
- All 5 medications (Lipitor, Ibuprofen, Aspirin, 
  Paracetamol, Penicillin) prescribed almost equally — 
  no single drug dominates treatment protocols

###  Financial & Insurance Insights-:
- Maximum single patient bill reached **$52,764** — 
  showing extreme cases driving revenue outliers
- **6,091 High Risk patients** (Emergency admission + 
  Abnormal test result) require priority resource allocation
- **Cigna** covers the most patients among all 5 providers
- Average billing is remarkably consistent across all 
  conditions ($25,161–$25,806) — suggesting standardized 
  pricing rather than condition-based billing
- **Obesity** patients cost insurers most with 
  highest avg billing of $26,117 under Cigna

###  Data Quality Finding-:
- **108 negative billing records** were identified and 
  removed during data cleaning — representing 0.19% 
  of total dataset
- Patient names had inconsistent casing (e.g., "Bobby 
  JacksOn") — standardized using Excel PROPER() function

###  Business Recommendations-:
1. **Create Asthma fast-discharge protocol** — 15.70 day 
   avg stay is highest; early discharge saves bed costs
2. **Prioritize Arthritis diagnostic resources** — 34.3% 
   abnormal rate means most patients need follow-up tests
3. **Build COVID surge capacity plan** — 2020 data proves 
   hospitals need 50%+ buffer capacity for health emergencies
4. **Review Obesity prevention programs** — at $25,806 avg 
   billing, chronic condition management is most expensive
5. **High Risk patient protocol** — 6,091 Emergency + 
   Abnormal cases need dedicated rapid response team
6. **Billing standardization review** — nearly identical 
   billing across all conditions ($25,161–$25,806) suggests 
   pricing may not reflect actual treatment complexity.
